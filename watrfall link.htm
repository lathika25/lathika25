These 4 Waterfalls In Araku Valley Are All About Serenity And Rejuvenation!

..
SHARES

23 Mar 2023

Written by

Seema Gurnani

Araku Valley is a very renowned and utmost beautiful hill station, let alone a valley region in the southeastern Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is well situated amidst the thick and dense forests of the Eastern Ghats mountain range. Araku Valley is well recommended for all types of travelers be it, family, with kids or couples at the same time. This is indeed a great holiday destination where several tourist attractions are available to be explored along with some naturally blessed waterfalls that are worth a visit. The below-mentioned waterfalls in Araku Valley are nothing less than refreshing. Take a look!

Top 4 Waterfalls In Araku Valley

Here is a list of waterfalls in Araku Valley that are definitely worth a visit. These falls never fail to reek of charm and magic. Take a look.

1. Katiki Waterfalls
2. Dumbriguda Waterfalls
3. Sangda Waterfalls
4. Chaparai Waterfalls

1. Katiki Waterfalls

Image Source

Katiki Waterfalls is one of the best waterfalls in Araku Valley which was named after Katiki and that is how it inherited the name in the first place. It is said to graciously set about 4 km away from Borra Caves and as far as its height is concerned, it is about 50 ft high. Its origin goes back to the mighty Gosthani River and its vicinity is full of greenery and very clear waters. Many adventure enthusiasts have found their haven here owing to its surroundings which got several trekking spots and they are simply perfect for trek lovers.

Operating hours:7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
Entry fees: Free

2. Dumbriguda Waterfalls

Image Source

Dumbriguda Waterfalls is one of the widely visited waterfalls in Araku Valley that are located about 15 km away and falls right on the way to Paderu. It is indeed a very fine attraction owing to its endless streams that graciously cut across big rock formations of Araku Valley. This spot is also perfect for family picnics and it is also considered to be very romantic where the beautiful stream spreading across the entire valley is said to have some magical vibes which simply makes the entire surrounding utmost romantic. As a matter of fact, this said spot is a simply perfect and most preferred location for Telugu filmmakers.

Operating hours:N/A
Entry fees: Free

3. Sangda Waterfalls

Image Source

Sangda Waterfalls is another one of the famous waterfalls in Araku Valley that is widely visited by tourists as well as locals throughout the year. It is very well situated in a small yet decently beautiful hamlet called Sangda and this village is a part of the famous Munchingput Mandal. Several couples are often seen loitering here in a pursuit to find and witness the perfect moment. Witnessing the waterfalls here and enjoying its charm doubles when you are visiting with your near and dear ones.

Operating hours:N/A
Entry fees: Free

4. Chaparai Waterfalls

Image Source

This is one of the amazing and utmost beautiful waterfalls of Araku Valley that is well located about 16 km away from Araku Valley. There is sliding down the string and the scenic yet picturesque views witnessed here are worth your time and money. There is amazing food served in nearby restaurants such as bamboo chicken and chicken kababs. This is indeed a great weekend gateway spot to have a great time with friends and family by virtue of being an absolutely mesmerizing charm and beauty.

Operating hours:N/A
Entry fees: Free

Araku Valley is a very beautiful and widely visited holiday destination in the heart of Andhra Pradesh. This place is truly known for its beauty in diversity. There are several places to visit and many adventure enthusiasts are known to find their haven here right in the heart of Araku Valley as the place is also quite reputed and recognized for its adventure activities. So, if you are looking for a decent place to have a good holiday with your near and dear ones then make sure you take a trip to Andhra Pradesh and make sure not to miss visiting the above-mentioned waterfalls in Araku Valley.

Frequently Asked Questions About Waterfalls In Araku Valley

Where is Araku Valley located?

Araku Valley is a very beautiful hill station located right in the southeastern Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.

Is Araku Valley worth visiting?

Yes, Araku Valley is a place worth visiting. There are several places to visit in its vicinity and each place and every attraction are worth your time and money. It is indeed a great place to visit with your near and dear ones as the place never tends to disappoint its visitors.

What is the best time to visit Araku Valley?

Araku Valley is visited throughout the year however the recommended time is said to be between October and March as during this period the sightseeing and outdoor activities can be best witnessed. To make a note of, winters in Araku Valley can be quite cool and the temperature may go down up to 10 degrees Celsius at night.

Which are the best waterfalls in Araku Valley?

Katiki Waterfalls and Dumbriguda Waterfalls are considered the best-known waterfalls in Araku Valley owing to its perfect location and decent environment. There are several things to do here such as picnicking and trekking besides watching the heavenly blessed waterfalls.

Is it safe to visit waterfalls in Araku Valley?

Yes, it is very safe to visit waterfalls in Araku Valley provided one should be careful while heading the said spots as the paths heading towards it may be rough at times due to weather and many other factors.
